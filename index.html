Track your XRP escrows and token balances — all in one place.
VAULT connects to the XRP Ledger and shows you exactly what's locked, what's available, and when it unlocks. Built for XRPL holders, developers, and anyone managing escrows.
  • Add one or more wallets by their public address
  • See your XRP balance and all token holdings instantly
  • Mark any balance as an escrow and set your own unlock timeline
  • Track countdowns, progress, and claimable amounts
Your privacy is protected. VAULT only needs your public wallet address — the r… address. No seed phrase, no private key. Nothing sensitive ever enters this app. All data is saved locally on your device only.
